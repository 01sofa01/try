Information Technology

Information Technology (IT) refers to the use of systems (computers and telecommunications) for storing, retrieving, and transmitting information. IT has revolutionized various industries through advanced tools and software applications. It connects the world through the internet, making global communication seamless.

History of IT

IT has its roots in the early computers of the 20th century and has evolved rapidly over the decades.

Modern Applications

IT is applied in various fields such as education, healthcare, finance, and entertainment.

Future Trends

The future of IT includes artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and blockchain technologies.
